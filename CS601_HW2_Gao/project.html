Project

Community Web Application

This is an open-source project which my team did last semester. I do not have much to describe myself so that I just put it down here.

Community is an application that allows residents to book times at the courts and inform other dog-owning neighbors that they are at the dog park via QR code. Community is also an application for local businesses to connect with its residents by paying to advertise their products and services as well as host events. The community is focused on trying to improve the local Boston area businesses and everyday life for its residents.
